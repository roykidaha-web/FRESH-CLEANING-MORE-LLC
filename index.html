Got questions?

We're always happy to talk through what you need. If you don't find your answer here, just give us a call.

What areas of Houston do you serve?
We're based at Hillcroft Park Center (5645 Hillcroft St #311) and serve Houston and surrounding areas. Call us to confirm we cover your neighborhood.
Do I need to prepare anything before the visit?
Just let us in! We bring all supplies and equipment. If you'd like to pick up loose clutter beforehand you're welcome to, but it's not required.
Do you offer recurring cleaning plans?
Yes — weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly. Recurring clients get priority scheduling and consistent service from the same trusted crew.
Is your team insured and background-checked?
Our team is professional, uniformed, and vetted. Every cleaning ends with a crew leader walk-through before we leave your home.
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice to reschedule. Call us at (832) 860-8241 and we'll find a time that works for you.
Do you clean apartments and condos?
Absolutely — studios, condos, townhomes, and houses of all sizes. Same level of care regardless of home type or size.
